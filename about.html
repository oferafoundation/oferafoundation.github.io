ABOUT

What We Do

The Ofera Foundation is an organization founded on the basis of helping everyone learn about what is currently happening to our environment. Members of Ofera strive to reach out to help save our planet to by raising money for charity, spreading awareness through articles, and providing easy access to volunteering opportunities.

Who we are

Ofera was started by a group of young, like-minded individuals. All hailing from Arcadia High School in Southern California, they each utilize their unique skill sets while working toward a common goal: creating something beneficial, useful, and significant. Together we hope to make the world a better place, one change at a time.
